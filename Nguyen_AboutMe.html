About Me

TinhPhong Nguyen

Why do we humans have goals in life? Hello, my name is TinhPhong, and I attend Dublin High School. My long-term goal is to become a successful Principal Software Engineer. My plan for reaching this goal is simple, but it will require diligence. I plan to graduate high school and average at least a 4.3 GPA. When I get into college, I will take the classes that I will need to graduate with a Computer Science Major, and I might even take extra classes in the business or economics field. After graduating from college, I will earn a job as a computer scientist, and continue my journey towards a happy life.

My strengths that relate to this career are ambition and independence. In all tests, assignments, and projects, I always am determined to get the highest score possible, and when I don't get the score that I want, I study and work harder to attain my desired score. My ambition will help me achieve this goal because when I have a desire for this career, I will work harder and harder until I get it. I am extremely independent because I do a lot of activities and work for myself, from doing the laundry, to washing the dishes, and even cooking. An independent mindset will help me achieve my goal because it will be one of the most important skills for a software engineer as every person with a job needs to be able to be responsible and diligent towards their work. Additionally, I can program applications with Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and MIT App Inventor. My ability to code and think through algorithms will definately help me succeed in the engineering field.

I have had many accomplishments. One of them is getting preety high grades in highschool, where my first B ever was in my Junior year. I have managed to obtain a 4.33 GPA in my Sophomore year, Junior year, and Senior year of Highschool along with a 4.0 in my Freshman year. An additional accomplishment of mine includes being a leader in a youth group for over two years. Also, I have helped develop the Vitenamese Student Association club, and I have been the Vice President of this club for over 3 semesters, and I am the current President. These accomplishments have taught me many things. My experience as a youth group leader taught me how effective a team could be with the use of good communication, while getting excellent grades taught me that working hard is worth it, and this is not because of the rewards earned, but because of the further opportunities that I obtain.

I have a goal to become a software engineer. I have many accomplishments throughout my life. Everything that I have experienced through life has taught me many things that make me the person I am today. My website represents who I am, so check out my other fascinating pages.

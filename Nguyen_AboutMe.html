About Me

TinhPhong Nguyen

Why do us as humans have goals in life? Hello, my name is TinhPhong, and I attend Dublin High School. My long-term goal is to become a successful entrepreneur who makes enough money to live... My plan for reaching this goal is simple. I will first graduate from high school and attempt to average higher than a 4.0 GPA. When I get into college, I will take the classes that I will need to graduate with a Computer Science Major, and maybe even take extra classes about creating your own business. After graduating college, I will create multiple online businesses where I buy, sell, and fix computer parts. This is my dream job because as an entrepreneur people have the potential to: make a lot of money; live a happy life; as well as learn a lot of new skills.

My strengths that relate to this career are ambition and independence. In all tests, assignments, and projects, I always am determined to get the highest score possible, and when I don't get the score that I want, I study and work harder to get my desired score. My ambition will help me achieve this goal because when I have a desire for this career, I will work harder and harder until I get it. I am extremely independent because I do a lot of things for myself, from doing the laundry, to washing the dishes, and even cooking. This independence will help me achieve my goal because it will be one of the most important skills for an entrepreneur since every entrepreneur creates their own business. You must know how to take care of yourself and do things by yourself before creating your own business.

I have had many accomplishments. One of them is getting a GPA of 4.0 and Principal's Honor Roll from 6th to 9th grade. Never getting a B makes me prideful and more ambitious to get all A's. Over the summer, I also packaged food for hungry kids all over Asia and Africa in a non-profit organization called Kids Against Hunger. Another accomplishment of mine is making the Wells 8th grade basketball team. There were about 40 kids that tried out for the team, and I was one of the 13 people who made the team. Besides making the basketball team, helping my team make the playoffs was a true accomplishment as well. All of these accomplishments have taught me many things. Making the basketball team taught me how effective a team could be if communication was good. Better communication made better executions of plays in basketball. Getting a 4.0 GPA for 3 years straight taught me that working hard is worth it. Not because of the rewards I get, but because it makes me feel good about myself as a person.

I have a goal to become an entrepreneur. I have many accomplishments throughout my life. Everything that I have experienced through life has taught me many things that make me the person I am today. My website represents who I am, so check out my other fascinating pages.
